Omelette

  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 3
butter 100g
Oil 100ml

    Step 1: Whisk the eggs together

    Step 2: Medium heat

    Step 3: Melt butter and oil in pan

    Step 4: Put eggs in pan

    Step 5: Cook until cooked

Toast

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
bread 2pc
cheddar cheese 2pc
Butter 100g

    Step 1: Spread butter on bread

    Step 2: Medium heat

    Step 3: Melt butter in pan

    Step 4: Put bread in pan

    Step 5: Put cheddar on bread

    Step 6: Put other bread on top

    Step 7: Cook until nicely colored

    Step 8: Cut in half diagonally
